## Project Overview

Development of a modern, professional website for Isaac Law LLP - a full-service law firm based in Nairobi, Kenya. The website showcases the firm's practice areas, team members, and provides seamless client communication through WhatsApp integration.

## Deliverables

### 1. Website Features
- ✅ Responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- ✅ Modern UI with custom branding (Gold #C6A772 & Teal #25414D)
- ✅ Dark/Light theme toggle
- ✅ Professional typography (Playfair Display & Inter fonts)
- ✅ SEO-optimized structure

### 2. Page Sections
- ✅ Hero section with call-to-action
- ✅ About section
- ✅ Practice Areas showcase (6 areas)
- ✅ Team profiles (4 members)
- ✅ Legal Insights/Blog section
- ✅ Contact section with WhatsApp integration
- ✅ Professional footer

### 3. Interactive Features
- ✅ Sticky navigation header
- ✅ Mobile-responsive menu
- ✅ WhatsApp floating widget
- ✅ Contact form with WhatsApp submission
- ✅ Smooth scrolling navigation
- ✅ Hover animations and transitions

### 4. Technical Specifications
- ✅ Built with Next.js 16 (React 19)
- ✅ TypeScript for type safety

- ✅ Optimized performance
- ✅ Cross-browser compatibility

### 5. Assets Included
